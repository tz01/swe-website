    Welcome to Cornell Engineering and the Cornell SWE family! Every year, we hold a SWEek of welcome where students can mingle with other students and upperclassmen and learn about opportunities at Cornell! Check out our Google calendar and Instagram for the schedule!
    The Cornell University Society of Women Engineers proudly presents our semiannual Professional Networking Dinner in the Statler Ballroom. Held the evening before Cornell's technical career fair, the Professional Networking Dinner enables company representatives and students to interact in an intimate yet relaxed environment.
